Project: Triggers in Movies

For some, certain images, events, or words, can invoke powerful emotions - and maybe ones we'd rather not experience. Or at least not be blindsided by while watching a movie. This simple website/app/algorthim is meant to help with that. Below, you'll find a place where you can search for a movie title and give the website a "trigger" word. On the backend, the app will perform a simple search of the movie's synopsis using IMDb's information for that word. I hope that this helps you in some way.
